Introduction to the Premier League

The Premier League is known around the world as the most competitive league in football. It consists of 20 teams playing 38 games over the course of a year, usually commencing in September. The team that wins the Premier League get the Premier League trophy and all players on the winning team recieve medals. The trophy is shown below.

The top four teams in the Premier League get to compete in next years Champions League against the other top teams from various leagues around the world. The bottom three teams at the end of the season get relegated to the Championship (not to be confused with the Champions League) and have to compete to win promotion back to the Premier League.

The last five winners of the Premier League are as follows:

  • 2019/2020 - Liverpool
  • 2018/2019 - Manchester City
  • 2017/2018 - Manchester City
  • 2016/2017 - Chelsea
  • 2015/2016 - Leicester City
