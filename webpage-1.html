Introduction to Leeds United

Leeds United are an English football team that currently play in the Premier League. They recently got promoted to the Premier league in 2020 after 16 years in the Championship. Their mananger at the moment is Argentinian legend Marcelo Bielsa who is the person responsible for thier recent promotion. For more information on the Premier League click on the link below.

More about Leeds United

Leeds United's home stadium is Elland Road, located just outside Leeds City Centre and just off the M621. Leeds supporters are known to fill the staduim for every home game and are considered to have one of the largest followings in the country.

Leeds normally play in a 4-1-4-1 formation with the positions filled as follows:

  • GK- Illan Meslier
  • RB- Luke Ayling
  • CB- Robin Koch
  • CB- Liam Cooper
  • LB- Stuart Dallas
  • CDM- Kalvin Phillips
  • RM- Helder Costa
  • CM- Mateuz Klich
  • CM- Pablo Hernandez
  • LM- Jack Harrison
  • ST- Patrick Bamford
